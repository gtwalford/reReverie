This is a generative experience using ProcessingJS - by Gabriel Walford.


To explore through the areas there are a couple keyboard commands you need to know:

'UP' arrow - moves Forward || 'DOWN' arrow - moves Backward
'LEFT' arrow - moves Left || 'RIGHT' arrow - moves Right
'r' - moves Upward || 'f' - moves Downward
'w' - angles Upward || 's' - angles Downward
'a' - angles Left || 'd' - angles Right

Hint: If the controls do not seem to be responding - click once on the window.
